## 🎨 Visual Indicators

### Status Badges:
- **Green**: Graded, Present, Good attendance (≥75%)
- **Blue**: Submitted, Pending
- **Yellow**: Warning, Late, Fair attendance (50-74%)
- **Orange**: Pending review, Action required
- **Red**: Overdue, Absent, Poor attendance (<50%)

### Icons Used:
- 📚 Books (Courses)
- 👥 People (Students)
- 📝 Document (Assignments)
- 📋 Clipboard (Submissions)
- ⭐ Star (Graded)
- 📅 Calendar (Schedule)
- 🔔 Bell (Alerts)
- 📊 Chart (Stats)

### Dynamic Today's Schedule:
- Shows different classes based on day of week
- Empty state on weekends
- Updates automatically

- "6 hours ago"
- "1 day ago"
- "28 days ago"

### Due Date Warnings:
- Green: Due in 7+ days
- Yellow: Due in 3-7 days
- Orange: Due in 1-3 days
- Red: Overdue
